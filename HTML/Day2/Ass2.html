About Paris

Paris is the capital of France and one of the most beautiful cities in the world. It is known as the "City of Light" and attracts millions of visitors every year.

The city is famous for its Eiffel Tower, art museums, fashion, and delicious food. People from all over the world come to see its historical landmarks and enjoy the unique culture.

Famous Places in Paris

The Louvre Museum is the world's largest art museum and holds the famous painting Mona Lisa. The Notre-Dame Cathedral is another wonderful attraction that shows the city's history and architecture.
